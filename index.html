About Me

My name is Rishi Manimaran and I am third year CS major from McLean, Virginia. I've always found great fulfillment in helping my community, and my experience as a branch manager for the GIVE tutoring program was especially rewarding. Working directly with underserved children, I saw firsthand the positive impact education can have on young lives. Now, I'm excited about the prospect of combining my passion for software development with my desire to help others. I envision a career where I can use my programming skills to create innovative solutions that address social issues, improve access to education, or streamline nonprofit operations. By merging technology with social impact, I hope to scale my ability to make a difference and contribute to meaningful projects that positively affect communities on a broader scale.
